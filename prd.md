# SevaLink - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Overview

SevaLink is a service marketplace platform that connects customers with verified local service providers.

The platform allows customers to browse available services, select verified providers, and book appointments online.

Service providers register on the platform and are verified by an administrator before becoming visible to customers.

The platform consists of three portals:

- Customer Website
- Service Provider Dashboard
- Admin Dashboard

Future versions will include a mobile application.

---

# Goals

- Provide an easy way to discover trusted local services.
- Verify all service providers before listing them.
- Simplify service booking.
- Give providers a professional dashboard.
- Give administrators complete control over the platform.
